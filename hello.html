Our Story

Kokum Rasa is a family-owned business founded in 2015 with a mission to bring traditional Indian beverages to modern consumers. Our recipe has been perfected over three generations, preserving the authentic taste of kokum while ensuring the highest quality standards.

We work directly with farmers in the Konkan region of Maharashtra, supporting sustainable agriculture and fair trade practices. Each bottle of Kokum Rasa represents our commitment to quality, tradition, and community.
